MoneyShot Mobile App and Website

Send a drink to a friend to be redeemed at a local bar

After retiring from professional fighting I took the drink board concept and stepped into the start-up arena. Bootstrapping the idea from napkin to mobile app my partner and I went from zero app experience to designing, overseeing development, and the marketing of MoneyShot.

Due to lack of funding we were not able to maintain traction and pay for further developments.
