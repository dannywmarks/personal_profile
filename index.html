Who I am

Martial Engineer, Software Artist, Blockchain Enthusiast, and Javascript Aficionado.

I am a lifelong martial artist with an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and a passion for technology. Since my childhood, I have always had an interest in the details of how things work.

After retiring from fighting professionally, I began teaching the martial arts while pursuing my interests in the tech industry. In 2014 I followed an idea for a startup that came from working as a bartender when I was younger. I bootstrapped my first startup company "MoneyShot" and was heavily involved in the mobile app's design process. MoneyShot allowed users to buy each other drinks at local bay area bars. Unfortunately, due to lack of funding, I was not able to continue development in the project.

Frustrated that MoneyShot development ended due to lack of funds, I took a more profound interest in the app development process and began learning to program. I began studying online but looked for a more straightforward path and joined the Springboard Software Engineering Bootcamp.

Through Springboard, I developed a deeper appreciation for computer science and engineering while learning cutting-edge technologies. As a result, I have taken a strong interest in cryptocurrency and blockchain development.

I am currently looking to join an engineering team to help further my development as a Software Engineer.
